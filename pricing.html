Frequently Asked Questions

Is there any contract?

There is no contract. You can cancel anytime. If you have subscribed to monthly billing using your Credit or Debit Card, you can login and delete your card. If you are paying manually using Paypal, simple ignore the invoice. You can also contact us for cancellation.

What are the payment options?

You can pay using Credit/Debit Card or Paypal. Once invoice is generated, you will be sent an email and you can pay by clicking the Pay via Credit or Debit Card button or Pay via Paypal in the invoice email. You can also subscribe to automated monthly payments using your Credit or Debit Card.

Is your system secure?

We employ various security systems to keep your data safe including using SSL, firewalls, and anti-virus solutions. All data is backed up regularly.
